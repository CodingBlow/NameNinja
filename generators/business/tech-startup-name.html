Tech Startup Naming Tips

Naming your tech business is a crucial step in establishing your brand identity. Consider these factors when evaluating potential names:

Digital Presence

Your name must be available as a domain, particularly .com. Also check for availability across all major social media platforms to maintain brand consistency.

Mobile-Friendly

Ensure your name is easy to type on mobile devices. Avoid complex spellings or letter combinations that could lead to errors when typing on smaller screens.

Voice Assistant Ready

With the rise of voice search, choose a name that's easy to pronounce and will be correctly interpreted by voice assistants like Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant.

Future-Proof

Avoid names that limit your future growth or expansion. Your name should allow you to pivot or expand your product offerings without becoming irrelevant.
