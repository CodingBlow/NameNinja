Creating Compelling Heroes

A memorable name is just one aspect of a great hero. Consider these tips when developing your protagonist:

Flaws & Vulnerabilities

Perfect heroes are boring. Give your protagonist weaknesses, fears, or moral dilemmas that create internal conflict.

Compelling Motivations

What drives your hero? Personal beliefs, past trauma, love, duty, or redemption can all provide strong motivational forces.

Character Growth

Heroes should evolve throughout their journey, learning from failures and changing their perspectives or abilities.

Meaningful Relationships

A hero's connections with allies, mentors, and even enemies help define their character and provide emotional depth.
