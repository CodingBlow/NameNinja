Creating Compelling Elven Characters

Elves are one of fantasy's most enduring races, with rich lore across many fictional worlds. Consider these aspects when developing your elven character:

Lifespan Perspective

How does your elf's long lifespan shape their worldview? Do they view shorter-lived races with curiosity, condescension, or compassion?

Cultural Connection

What aspects of traditional elven society does your character embrace or reject? Are they deeply traditional or a maverick within their culture?

Magical Affinity

How does your elf relate to magic? Are they naturally gifted, studiously trained, or perhaps unusual in lacking magical ability?

Personal History

What significant events in your elf's potentially centuries-long life have shaped them? What historic events have they witnessed firsthand?
