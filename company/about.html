About Name Fabulous

Helping people find the perfect names since 2020

Our Story

Name Fabulous was born from a simple observation: finding the perfect name is often challenging, whether it's for a new baby, a beloved pet, a fictional character, or a business venture.

Our founder, Alex Chen, experienced this firsthand when struggling to name both a new startup and a rescue puppy in the same month. After hours of searching through endless lists and brainstorming sessions, Alex realized there was an opportunity to create something better.

In 2020, we launched with just two name generators. Today, we've expanded to offer specialized tools for multiple naming needs, helping millions of users worldwide find names that resonate with them.

What began as a side project has grown into a passionate team of naming enthusiasts, linguists, data scientists, and creative writers—all dedicated to making the naming process easier, more enjoyable, and more meaningful for our users.

Our Mission

"We believe that names matter. They shape identity, create first impressions, and carry meaning. Our mission is to help people find names that truly resonate—names that feel right and carry the perfect meaning."

Inspiration

We aim to inspire creativity and help users discover options they might not have considered.

Education

We provide context, history, and meaning behind names to enable informed choices.

Diversity

We celebrate naming traditions from cultures around the world, expanding horizons beyond the familiar.

Utility

We create practical tools that simplify the naming process and save users time and effort.
