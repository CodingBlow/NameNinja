Frequently Asked Questions

How do your name generators work?

Our name generators use sophisticated algorithms combined with curated name databases to create personalized name suggestions. Each generator is tailored to its specific purpose (baby names, pet names, etc.) and allows customization through various filters like gender, style, origin, and more.

Are the generated names unique?

While our generators create a wide variety of name combinations, we cannot guarantee complete uniqueness for every name. However, many generators (like the Startup Name Generator) include options for highly unique, made-up names that are more likely to be one-of-a-kind.

Can I save my favorite names?

Currently, the free version doesn't include a save feature. However, you can easily bookmark or write down your favorite options. Our premium version (coming soon) will include features to save favorite names and export name lists.

Do you offer name meanings and origins?

Yes, many of our generators (particularly the Baby Name Generator) include information about name meanings and origins when available. This helps you choose names with significance that resonates with your preferences.
